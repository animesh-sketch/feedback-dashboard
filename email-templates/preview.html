Key Insight

Mid-tier churn increased by 0.8pp despite overall revenue growth — primarily driven by customers on the legacy pricing plan. Recommend a targeted retention campaign before Q2.

Top Findings
  • 01 Organic search drove 38% of new signups in February — up from 24% in January, likely due to the SEO changes shipped on Feb 3rd.
  • 02 Mid-tier churn rose to 3.2%. Exit survey data points to pricing as the primary reason (mentioned in 61% of responses).
  • 03 Support ticket volume dropped 18% after the onboarding flow update — reducing cost-to-serve by an estimated $4,200/month.
  • 04 Mobile sessions now account for 52% of all traffic. Dashboard load time on mobile is still 3.4s — recommend optimisation in March.
